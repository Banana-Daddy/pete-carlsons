● 03 · Story

Forty-five seasons
in the desert.

Pete and Edna Carlson opened the shop on September 10, 1981. Pete learned the trade in Oakland under Don Sherwood and followed him out to Palm Desert at the tail end of the 1970s. A year later he opened his own store a few doors down. That store has not moved since.

They started with golf. Tennis followed. Pickleball followed that. Today it's the only full-line racquet and club shop in the Coachella Valley — a 14,000-square-foot specialty against two PGA Tour Superstores within a mile. Same family. Same block. Five decades.
